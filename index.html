About Me

Hello! I'm a Machine Learning Developer

Hi, I’m Alexandru, a Machine Learning Developer with a passion for turning complex data into meaningful solutions. My journey started when I built my first object detection model with YOLO in university, and I’ve been hooked on the challenge of solving real-world problems with AI ever since.

I enjoy working at the intersection of machine learning and software development, where I can bring ideas from research into practical, user-friendly applications. Whether it’s experimenting with neural networks, deploying models into production, or optimizing performance, I love the mix of creativity and engineering.

Outside of coding, I constantly educate myself through research papers, online courses, and exploring design principles to understand how people interact with technology. I’m passionate about building solutions that make life smarter and simpler.
