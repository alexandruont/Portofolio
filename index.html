About Me

Hello! I'm a Machine Learning Engineer

Hi, I'm Alexandru, a Machine Learning Engineer with a Computer Science background from the Technical University of Cluj-Napoca. My focus is computer vision: for my thesis I built PARCAT, an ANPR parking-enforcement system running YOLOv8 on an NVIDIA Jetson — trained, optimized and deployed end-to-end.

I work across the full ML lifecycle — training, optimization and deployment, including on NVIDIA Jetson devices, backend APIs with FastAPI and containerized infrastructure with Docker. Lately I've also been building LLM-powered applications: RAG pipelines, vector search and agent-based systems.

Outside of work, I keep learning through research papers and online courses and enjoy turning what I learn into side projects that make everyday life smarter and simpler.
