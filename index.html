About Me

Hello! I'm a Machine Learning Developer

I'm passionate about leveraging the power of machine learning to solve real-world problems. With a strong background in data science and a growing interest in web development, I enjoy creating end-to-end solutions that bridge the gap between complex algorithms and user-friendly interfaces.

My journey in tech started with a fascination for data patterns and has evolved into building scalable ML models and deploying them through modern web applications. I'm always eager to learn new technologies and tackle challenging problems.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring the latest ML research papers, contributing to open-source projects, or experimenting with new web frameworks.
